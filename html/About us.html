About Us


Star Film Awards 2021 powered by SLT Mobitel
Star Film Awards 2021 is Sri Lanka’s premier and most popular event of the year
to encourage people involved in the film industry.
This ceremony was first established in 2002 and
since the begining, production crew had the ability to hold this annually.

The most important part of this program is to select the most popular actor and
actress by peoples’ votes. We present this online voting website that we have set
up to express the people's preference. Throughout this period, there are a lot of
positive comments from the people about this motivational ceremony.
This helped us to encourage our organizing teams as well.

SF Awards proudly announce that this is the only film awarding ceremony in the region that organized since couple of decades. No doubt that both artists and movie fans will welcome this move to evaluate the movies while they are still live in good memories of them, despite the challenges in evaluating and organizing the event in such a short span of time.
