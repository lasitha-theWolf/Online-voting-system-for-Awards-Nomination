Conference – Star Film Awards 2021




Press conference on eleventh encounter of Star Film Awards held on 22th May 2021 at the BMICH. The Nominations Evening to announce nominees for awards will be held on 25th April 2020 at Avenra Gangara Wennappuwa. The Eleventh encounter of Star Film Awards, which is also the biggest annual event of the film industry in Sri Lanka will be held on the 25st of May 2020 at the BMICH. Following participants were at the press conference head table;

  • Mr. Chandima Fernando - Chief Executive Officer - SLT Mobitel
  • Mr. Pujitha Rajapaksa - Senior Manager Marketing – Sampath Bank
  • Dr. Ravi Liyanage - Chairman - Unilever PLC
  • Mrs. Shirani Amarasinghe - Senior Director - MAS Holdings
  • Mr. Buddadasa Galappatty - Chairman Panel of Judges


Entries are now accepted for the festival.
Star Film Awards proudly announce that this is the only film awarding ceremony in the region held within the 1st quarter immediately after the end of year under review. No doubt that both artists and television viewers will welcome this move to evaluate the TV programme while they are still live in good memories of them, despite the challenges in evaluating and organizing the event in such a short span of time. Continuation of an award ceremony at the highest level for sixteen consecutive years, overcoming many challenges is a very special victory for us and we are pleased to see that today the Raigam Tele’es has become a very special event of all stakeholders of the industry. We share that happiness and the strength we have thus gained with the media and the general public and we gladly and proudly accept the challenge of organizing the “Awards Ceremony of the Nation” to encompass the whole of the tele-media.

The Jury



The Panel of Judges consists of ten members and most of them have past experience in similar capacity. This time the Chairman of the panel is Mr. Buddadasa Galapattie, who is a well-known personality in the field of media, literacy and arts.thne. Further a well-known personality in the field of Tamil media will perform duties as special advisor in the Tamil medium. Awards will be presented under four main categories as: Television Media Excellence Awards

  • Awards of Appreciation
  • Most Popular Awards
  • Appreciation TV Channels
